Help us preserve Dr. Matilda A. Evans’ legacy and expand opportunity through education, public history, and community programs.

If you’d like to volunteer your time or explore a partnership, we’d love to hear from you:

- **Reach out**: [Contact the Foundation](/contact)

## Volunteer with the Foundation

Volunteers help us do the day-to-day work that keeps the mission moving. Opportunities can be one-time or ongoing.

You might be a good fit if you enjoy:

- Supporting events and community programs
- Assisting with research, archives, or transcription
- Helping organize photos, documents, and historical materials
- Supporting communications (writing, editing, social media, design)
- Education and outreach (schools, student groups, community partners)

## Partner with Us

We welcome partnerships with organizations aligned with the Foundation’s mission—especially schools, cultural institutions, researchers, and community groups.

Partnerships might include:

- Co-hosted programs, lectures, or community conversations
- Educational collaborations (curriculum, student projects, internships)
- Sponsorship of programming or preservation-related work
- Research collaborations and public history projects

## What to include in your message

To help us respond quickly, please include:

- Your name and organization (if applicable)
- How you’d like to help (volunteer, partner, sponsor, research, etc.)
- Your general availability or timeline
- Any relevant links (website, portfolio, program info)

## Next steps

We’ll review your note and follow up to schedule a short conversation.

- **Start here**: [Contact the Foundation](/contact)
