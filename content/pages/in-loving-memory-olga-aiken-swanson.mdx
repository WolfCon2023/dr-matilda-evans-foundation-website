Olga Mauvene Aiken Swanson (December 7, 1949 – March 26th, 2023), one of the
four grandchildren of Dr. Matilda A. Evans and beloved sister to Beverly
Aiken-Muhammad and James Evans Aiken and first cousin to Mary Agnes Evans
Smalls. Upon her passing, she requested that her body be donated to The
University of Texas in the name of her grandmother, Dr. Matilda A. Evans, for
further research in science.
