Breast Cancer Awareness online seminars to be conducted by certified and licensed registered nurses and other health practitioners who specialize in coaching cancer diagnosed women. The foundation desires to present the Breast Cancer seminars as an opportunity to demystify the disease and eradicate the stigma of breast cancer.
