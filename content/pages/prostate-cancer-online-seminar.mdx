Prostate Cancer online seminars to be conducted by certified and licensed registered nurses and other health practitioners who specialize in coaching cancer diagnosed men. The foundation desires to present the Prostate Cancer seminars as an opportunity to demystify the disease and eradicate the stigma of Prostate Cancer.
