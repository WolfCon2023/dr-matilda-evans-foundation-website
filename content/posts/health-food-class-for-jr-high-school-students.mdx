**THE DR. MATILDA A. EVANS’ CLASS**

## The Best Bean on Earth!

One of the most widely acclaimed foods in the world is so tiny it can fit on your fingernail. Yet it has been hailed for centuries as the bulwark of nutrition.

I speak none other than of the **Navy Pea Bean (_Phaseolus vulgaris_)**.

## Quick facts about the Navy Pea Bean

<ul className="not-prose mt-4 grid gap-3">
  <Fact icon={Globe}>
    The Navy Bean has its origin in Peru, South America thousands of years ago.
  </Fact>
  <Fact icon={Anchor}>
    The United States Navy started serving Navy Beans in the mid 1800’s to enlisted sailors as a
    staple because of the great nutritional value. This is how the bean got its name “Navy Pea
    Bean”.
  </Fact>
  <Fact icon={Waves}>
    The Navy Pea Bean helps the sailors to maintain energy levels during long periods of time spent
    under water. Additionally, this bean is high in protein and carbohydrates.
  </Fact>
  <Fact icon={Wheat}>
    Because of the high fiber content, Navy Beans digest more slowly in the body for sustained
    energy.
  </Fact>
  <Fact icon={Timer}>
    Navy Beans are also low in calories, yet high in fiber, thus great for weight loss because they
    are filling, causing you to eat less throughout the day.
  </Fact>
  <Fact icon={Landmark}>
    The United States Senate Bean Soup is served in the dining room of the United States Senate
    every day. It is a tradition that dates back to the early years of the 20th century. In 1940 a
    bowl of Navy Bean Soup was only fifteen cents.
  </Fact>
  <Fact icon={Shield}>
    During World War II, Australia served Navy Beans to troops. The bean was popular and were
    referred to as “Yankee Beans”.
  </Fact>
  <Fact icon={Building2}>
    Navy Beans are still a key ingredient for the world famous “Boston Baked Beans” recipe, fondly
    named after the city of Boston, Mass. This recipe became so popular that the city became known
    as “Bean Town”.
  </Fact>
  <Fact icon={MoonStar}>
    In the mid 1930’s the Nation of Islam was encouraged by The Honorable Elijah Muhammad to add the
    Navy Pea Bean as a stable soup to his followers. In fact, he created the Navy Bean Pie as a
    healthier alternative to the more known sweet potato pie.
  </Fact>
</ul>
